<p><strong>Agrihan</strong> is a dormant volcanic island located at latitude 18 46&#8217;N, longitude 145 40&#8217;E, approximately 390 km north of Saipan. It is 10 km long and 7 km wide with an area of approximately 47 km2 and a maximum elevation of 965 m. It is the highest point in CNMI and the largest single volcano.</p>
